  • APIdays Global 2018

    Bridging the Gap: Applying Lessons from API products to Healthcare Enterprise Integrations

    Paris, December 2018

    Leading the integrations team at a fast growth startup that lives at the intersection of healthcare and social services, I have worked with clients on both the payor and provider sides of the industry. Each of these groups have different resources and approaches to technology and business, but both are struggling to adopt modern API practices. This talk covers what I wish I’d known before trying to build out an API program at a startup serving these enterprise customers with complex, highly customized, on-prem legacy IT systems.
